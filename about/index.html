Who We Are

Building Simple, Powerful Tools for a Smarter Web

Toolzy was built by passionate software engineers who were tired of slow, bloated, and complicated online tools. We noticed that everyday tasks like converting files, optimizing images, or using developer utilities often required signups, paid plans, or insecure platforms. That’s where Toolzy was born.

Our Solution
