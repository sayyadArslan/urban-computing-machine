Welcome to Toolzy Help Center – your ultimate guide to getting the most out of our free online tools. At Toolzy, we provide a wide range of user-friendly utilities for working with images, videos, audio, URLs, text, and more. Whether you're resizing an image, trimming a video, generating a QR code, or downloading a social media thumbnail, Toolzy is designed to make the process simple and fast — no sign-up required. To use any of our tools, simply navigate to the appropriate page from the menu or use the search bar to find what you need. Each tool includes a clear interface where you can either upload a file, paste a link, or input text. Once you've provided the required input, you can customize settings such as size, format, or quality. When your task is complete, you’ll be able to preview the output, download the result to your device, or copy it to your clipboard. If you're having trouble with a file not uploading or a tool not functioning correctly, please make sure you're using a supported file type and that the file size is within the tool’s limits. Refreshing the page or using a different browser can also resolve many issues. Toolzy works on all modern devices and is mobile-friendly, so you can use it on the go with ease.We’re committed to continuously improving Toolzy and value user feedback. If you have suggestions for new tools, features, or notice any bugs, please don’t hesitate to reach out through our Contact page or email us at support@toolzy.com. We're always looking for ways to make Toolzy better for our users. Thank you for using Toolzy — your trusted online toolkit!
