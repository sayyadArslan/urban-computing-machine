Toolzy:All Email Tools in one Place

Toolzy Email Tools provide a fast, secure, and easy way to manage all your email-related tasks. From generating temporary emails, verifying email addresses, checking email formats, to sending test emails, these tools simplify email management for both personal and professional use.

Email Extractor

Email Extractor quickly finds and collects email addresses from websites, text, or documents for easy use.

Email Validator

Email Validator instantly checks email addresses for correctness and deliverability to ensure valid contacts.

Mailto Link

Mailto Link Generator creates clickable email links [`mailto:`] so users can easily send emails directly from your website.

UTM Link Generator

UTM Link Generator helps you create trackable URLs with UTM parameters to monitor your marketing campaigns effectively.
