Welcome to the Toolzy Help Center

Find answers, guides, and tutorials to make the most out of Toolzy’s free online tools.

What is Toolzy?
Toolzy is an all-in-one online toolkit that provides free, easy-to-use web-based tools for everyday needs — from file converters to image editing, social media utilities, and developer tools.
Is Toolzy free to use?
Yes, Toolzy is completely free to use. You don’t need to pay for any tool.
Do I need an account to use Toolzy?
No, you can use all the tools without creating an account.
Is Toolzy safe and secure?
Yes, Toolzy is safe. Files are processed securely in your browser or on our servers, and we don’t store personal files.
Can I compress multiple images at once?
Yes, Toolzy supports bulk image compression. Upload multiple images and compress them together.
How to resize an image without losing quality?
Use Toolzy’s Image Resizer tool. Upload your image, set the dimensions, and it will resize while keeping the best possible quality.
Tool not working, what should I do?
Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If issues persist, contact us via the Contact Page with details.
